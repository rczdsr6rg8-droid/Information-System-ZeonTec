INFORMATION SYSTEM FOR ACCOUNTING OF EQUIPMENT AND MATERIALS FOR CONSTRUCTION
Product made by family "ZEONTEK" technologies, by student college mgimo: Kopylov Alexey R.
INFO
The purpose of the system development is to automate key processes of construction companies. The solution allows you to effectively account for equipment and materials, minimize the time spent on resource management, speed up reporting, and increase the transparency of business processes. The system provides simple and convenient interaction with equipment, materials, orders, and analytics for all transactions related to your company's tangible assets.
POTENTIAL
  • Registration of the receipt of equipment and materials.
  • Accounting for their movement between the warehouse and facilities.
  • Balance control and timely replenishment of stocks.
  • Reporting for the company's management.
  • Reporting for the company's management.
  • Improving the efficiency of managing the material resources of a construction company.
